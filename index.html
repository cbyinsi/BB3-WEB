1) Scope

This policy applies to personal information processed in connection with the Service. This policy does not apply to third‑party websites, services, or content that we do not control. Their privacy practices are governed by their own policies.

2) Information We Collect

A. Information you provide

  • Notes content. Text, images, attachments, tags, and other content you intentionally save in BluBox Note. Notes are typically stored on your device and/or in your chosen backup/sync destination if you enable such features.
  • Account details (if you create an account). Name or display name, email address, authentication credentials, and settings.
  • Support communications. The content of messages you send to us (e.g., bug reports, feedback) and related contact information.

B. Information collected automatically

  • Device and app information. Device model, OS version, app version, language, and regional settings.
  • Usage data. Interactions within the app (e.g., feature taps, screens viewed), timestamps, and performance metrics (e.g., crashes, load times). We use this to improve reliability and user experience.
  • Log and diagnostics data. Error logs and diagnostic information generated when the app encounters problems.

C. Information from third parties

If you choose to connect or back up to a third‑party service (for example, a cloud storage provider), that service may share limited information with us per your settings and their privacy policy.

3) How We Use Information

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the Service.
  • Sync or back up notes if you enable such features.
  • Improve and personalize the Service, including analytics and research to enhance stability, performance, and usability.
  • Communicate with you about updates, security alerts, and support responses.
  • Detect, prevent, and address fraud, abuse, and security incidents; comply with legal obligations.
  • With your consent, for any other purpose described at the time of collection.

4) When We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information as follows:

  • Service providers. With vendors who process data on our behalf (e.g., cloud hosting, analytics, crash reporting) under contracts that require them to protect your information and use it only for our instructions.
  • Legal and safety. When we believe disclosure is necessary to comply with the law, respond to lawful requests, or protect the rights, property, or safety of BluBox Note, our users, or the public.
  • Business transfers. In connection with any merger, sale, financing, or acquisition of all or part of our business, subject to standard confidentiality safeguards.
  • With your direction or consent. For example, when you choose to export or share a note.
